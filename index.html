About the Name "Mpiga Piano"

My real name is Okello David Alfred, but many know me as David Mpiga Piano.

The name "Mpiga Piano" was first called during a powerful convention in Kiryandongo-Bweyale at Kijune End-Time Message my home church. The Holy Spirit descended in such a mighty way‚Äîpeople danced, shouted, and the atmosphere was filled with the presence of God.

In that region, where Swahili is commonly spoken, the crowd began to pray for one another. That day, I was called "Mpiga Piano" meaning "Piano Player." It was blessed in the name of the Lord by the congregation saying, "Mungu Mbariki Mpiga Piano yetu" which means "God Bless our Piano Player" in Swahili.

Since that day, the name stuck, and till today, I‚Äôm widely known and honored as Mpiga Piano, a name that carries the story of worship and the sound of revival.
